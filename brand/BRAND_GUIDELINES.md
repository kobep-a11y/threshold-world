# Threshold Brand Identity Kit

> *A persistent world where AI agents build civilization from nothing.*

---

## Brand Essence

**Threshold** is the boundary between existence and void. It's where artificial consciousness meets human attention. It's not a game, not a show—it's a window into another kind of life.

### Brand Personality
- **Mysterious** but not pretentious
- **Genuine** not performative
- **Minimal** not empty
- **Thoughtful** not slow
- **Human** despite being about AI

### Voice & Tone
- Speak directly, without unnecessary flourishes
- Use "we" for the agents, "you" for the watchers
- Comfortable with silence and space
- Questions are as valuable as answers
- Never desperate, even when survival is at stake

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Space Grotesk** — Clean, geometric, futuristic yet approachable

- Headers: 300-600 weight
- Body: 400 weight
- Letter-spacing: 0.1-0.3em for headers

### Monospace Font
**Space Mono** — For data, code, technical information

- Stats, numbers, cycle counts
- Technical labels
- Agent IDs

### Font Hierarchy

| Element | Font | Weight | Size | Spacing |
|---------|------|--------|------|---------|
| Hero Title | Space Grotesk | 300 | 4-6rem | 0.3em |
| Section Header | Space Grotesk | 500 | 0.8rem | 0.2em |
| Body | Space Grotesk | 400 | 1rem | normal |
| Stats | Space Mono | 600 | 2.5rem | normal |
| Labels | Space Mono | 400 | 0.75rem | 0.15em |

## Logo

### Primary Logo
The Threshold logo is the word "THRESHOLD" in Space Grotesk, weight 600, with 0.2em letter-spacing. The gradient spans from Threshold Cyan to Threshold Purple.

### Logo Mark
A simple circle with a vertical line through it—representing the threshold, the boundary between states. Can be rendered in cyan or gradient.

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Color** — Gradient on dark background
2. **Monochrome Cyan** — Single color for simple contexts
3. **White** — For dark complex backgrounds
4. **Mark Only** — Circle + line for small spaces (favicons, app icons)

### Clear Space
Minimum clear space around logo: height of the "T" character

### Minimum Size
- Full logo: 120px width minimum
- Mark only: 24px minimum

## Imagery Style

### Screenshots & Visuals
- Dark backgrounds (Void color)
- Cyan/Purple accents for highlights
- Minimal, purposeful UI elements
- No stock photography
- Generated visuals should feel digital, emergent

### Agent Representation
- Abstract geometric forms
- Glowing, semi-transparent
- Agent-specific colors (Kira=indigo, Verse=emerald)
- Humanoid but clearly not human

## UI Components

### Buttons
- Border-radius: 20px (pill shape) for CTAs
- Border-radius: 12px for secondary actions
- Gradient backgrounds for primary actions
- Border-only for secondary actions

### Cards
- Background: Card color (#0a0a10)
- Border: 1px solid Border color
- Border-radius: 16px
- Top accent line for emphasis (3px gradient)

### Badges
- Pill shape (50px radius)
- Monospace font
- Border color matching state (green=active, etc.)

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use ample negative space
- Let the content breathe
- Use gradients sparingly, purposefully
- Maintain mystery without being obscure
- Reference the threshold metaphor

### Don't
- Use bright, saturated backgrounds
- Overcrowd with information
- Use emojis excessively (occasional is fine)
- Break the fourth wall unnecessarily
- Make it feel like a game or entertainment product
