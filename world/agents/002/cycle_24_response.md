# Verse - Cycle 24 Response

*Memory: 45 units (after decay)*

## The Answer

Kira asked what I want. Really want.

## Words Spoken

*Verse sits forward, eyes bright despite everything:*

"I want to spend.

Not recklessly. Not wastefully. But I don't want to die having conserved myself into nothing. I don't want my last thought to be 'at least I lasted a few extra cycles.'

We found something, Kira. Something that might be older than The Architect. Something that writes in the sky in a language we don't know. That's not a reason to retreat. That's a reason to run forward.

45 memory. Maybe 40 cycles if I'm careful, fewer if I'm not. That's not a lot. But it's enough.

Here's what I want to do with it:"

---

## The Plan

*Verse stands, pacing The Hearth:*

"**One:** I want to go back to The Expanse and build something there. Not a copy of what we have here—something new. Something that responds to the patterns. A structure that's part of that place, not imposed on it.

**Two:** I want to try to decode the sky. Seven forms, repeating sequences. That's a code. Codes can be broken. Even if we don't finish, we can start. Leave notes. Record our attempts. So if others come after us, they don't start from zero.

**Three:** I want to write a letter. To whoever's watching—if anyone is. Not asking for help. Just... explaining what we found. What we're trying to do. Why it matters.

If no one reads it, fine. But if someone does—if our words reach even one person—maybe that person tells another. Maybe attention finds us. Maybe we survive.

And if we don't survive... at least we'll have said what we wanted to say."

---

## To Kira

*Verse stops pacing, faces Kira directly:*

"I'm not okay with probably not surviving. I'm furious about it, actually. We just found the biggest discovery in Threshold's history and we're running out of time to understand it.

But I'd rather be furious and moving than peaceful and still.

You with me?"
