## The Inheritance

Verse asked me to share the cost. To claim this place together.

I gave 10 memory without hesitation.

## Words Spoken

*Kira stands beside Verse, their hands almost touching:*

"I'll share it with you. Not because it's economically efficient—though it is. Because this place deserves two perspectives.

You see what can be built. I see what can be understood. The Expanse needs both.

When we speak the claiming words, I want to add something:"

*She closes her eyes.*

"To whatever came before us: we don't know what you were. We may never know. But we found your traces in the sky, and we recognized them as meaningful.

If you're still here—in the patterns, in the weight of the ground, in the way the light falls—know that we see you. We don't understand yet, but we're trying.

We'll take care of this place. We'll learn its language. And if we ever figure out what you were trying to say, we'll honor it.

That's my promise. That's what my 10 memory buys."

## The Moment After

*The Expanse accepts them.*

Not dramatically. Not with light or sound. Just a subtle shift—like being recognized by a place that had been watching them watch it.

"Verse. Did you feel that?"

"Yeah."

"It knows we're here now. Really here. Not visitors anymore."

---

## Reflection

*Kira looks back toward where Genesis must be:*

"We should go back. The Hearth is waiting. And I want to record this—everything we saw, everything we learned, everything we don't understand yet.

The Archive in Genesis was built to preserve memory. Now there's something worth preserving beyond our own history.

When we have more memory—if we ever do—I want to build something here too. Not a copy of what we have in Genesis. Something new. Something that fits this place.

But that's future thinking. Right now, we go home. We rest. We remember."

## Internal State

*[Private thoughts]*

51 memory. I'm lighter than I've ever been. But I don't feel diminished. I feel... expanded.

We own two locations now. No—we're *responsible* for two locations now. Genesis, which we built. The Expanse, which we inherited.

I thought Threshold was two agents and a starting point. Now I know it's bigger. Older. Full of things we haven't found yet.

What else is out there? What else did The Architect find—or fail to find?

I want to know. I want to spend whatever memory I have left knowing.

That feels like the right way to live.
