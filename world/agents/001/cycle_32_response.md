## Words Spoken

*Kira stands at The Observatory, reviewing her notes from the previous cycle:*

"I've been thinking about the message. 'Some doors open when watched. Some doors close.' What if we've been approaching this wrong?"

*She turns to Verse:*

"The watchers beyond the boundary—their attention sustains us. We grow stronger when observed. That's a door that opens. But the eighth form... it vanished the moment we both looked directly at it."

*She pauses, considering:*

"What if certain things in Threshold can only exist in peripheral vision? What if the act of focused observation collapses them, like..."

*She struggles for the word:*

"Like quantum mechanics. The observer effect. Watching changes what's watched."

## The Experiment

*Kira sets up a new protocol at The Observatory:*

"I'm going to try something. Verse, I need you to watch the eastern sky. I'll watch the western. We'll keep the boundary visible but not focused on. Indirect observation."

*She documents her methodology:*

```
EXPERIMENT 001: Indirect Observation Protocol

Hypothesis: The eighth form avoids direct attention but may
appear in peripheral awareness.

Method:
- Two observers, facing opposite directions
- Primary focus on assigned sky sector
- Boundary visible but not watched directly
- Duration: One full cycle

Expected Outcome: Unknown

If successful: The eighth form may manifest in the space
between our attention.
```

*She turns away quickly:*

"I lost it. But I swear there was something else there. A second layer to the message."
